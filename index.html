Our Heritage

A Fusion of Sri Lankan Legacy & Contemporary Gastronomy

Zentro Fab celebrates the rich tapestry of Sri Lankan culinary heritage, elegantly reimagined through modern gastronomic art.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to elevate authentic island spices—from pure Ceylon cinnamon to hand-ground roasted curries—into high-end global dining masterpieces. Under the guidance of our culinary visionaries, we seamlessly fuse local soul with premium ingredients, creating an unparalleled gastronomic journey.
